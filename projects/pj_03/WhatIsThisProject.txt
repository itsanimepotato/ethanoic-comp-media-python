This project is an art program, where I tried to expand my use of pixels. I originally started off with the background, which is the "smooth" background described in my code. However, I also wanted to have elements of code that I have made over the semester as a last project of sorts (as this is my last project at Stuyvesant High School). It shows the story of two sides, the gray and the color. The gray symbolizes the monotony of school, like how hard Stuyvesant is, but the color symbolizes the bonds I have made here, from extracurriculars, friends from class, etc. By pressing C, you will be able to see a circle rotating around the center, which is how each day passes: basically the same but a little different each day. By pressing S, you will be able to see a scribble, which represents people, where everyone is different. By pressing F, it shows how people are able to change from sad and lonely to bright and colorful, allowing for color to return back to the canvas (or how people change from bright and colorful to sad and lonely). By pressing L, you are able to see how students "conform" and behave for themselves and other people. By pressing 1, you are able to go back to a clean slate, symbolizing coming or leaving Stuyvesant High School and going to a new place.
